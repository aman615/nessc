Resources Related to Sikhism

You can learn more about Sikhism by visitng the following links:

Sikhism

  • Shiromani Gurdwara Parbandhak Committee Official Website of Shiromani Gurdwara Parbandhak Committee, Sri Amritsar
  • Guru Granth Darpan Punjabi translation of Sri Guru Granth Sahib along with commentary and word meanings by Prof. Sahib Singh.
  • Sri Granth Sri Guru Granth Sahib search engine and resources.
  • Sikhism Home Page Multi-category resources on Sikhism.
  • Sikhi To The Max A powerful gurbani search engine with English and Gurmukhi keywords.
  • Sikhism - BBC World Service Religion BBC's religion and ethics homepage on Sikhism.
  • Sikhnet Large repository of resources and keertan (sacred music).
  • Gurmat Sangeet Project Excellent collection of gurmat sangeet.
  • All About Sikhs Good information resources.
  • Amrit Bani Online Gurbani Radio/Stream.
  • Proud To Be Sikh Read/research Guru Granth Sahib online page by page.
  • Punjabonline Online Punjabi to English dictionary and vice versa.
  • Virsa Wear Merchandise on Sikhism and Punjabi.
  • SikhiWiki A free encyclopedia on Sikhism.
    • Still need more information? Run a Google search for Sikh-related web pages

Sikh News

  • SikhSangat Daily Sikh news.
  • Panthic Weekly Sikh news.
  • Sikh Spectrum Quarterly Sikh thought and commentary.

Sikh Associations

  • Sikh Coalition A community-based organization that defends civil rights and liberties for Sikhs in the United States.
  • Sikh American Legal Defense and Education Fund Resources on legal assistance, legislative advocacy and media relations for American Sikhs.
  • Sikh Association of Boston University Created for awareness of Sikhism on campus at Boston University as well as in Boston.
  • United Sikhs A non-profit organization that empowers minority communities across the world.
  • Sikh Research Institute A non-profit organization that facilitates training and development while inspiring Sikh values.
  • Ensaaf Works to end impunity and achieve justice for mass state crimes in Punjab.
